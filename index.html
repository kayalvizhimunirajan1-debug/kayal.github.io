Our Galactic Home

The Milky Way is a barred spiral galaxy containing 100–400 billion stars, including our Sun. Sprawling across 100,000 light-years, it's a cosmic wonder. From glowing nebulae to mysterious black holes, the galaxy invites endless curiosity. At BigPlanetarium, we bring the universe closer with simulation and wonder.
